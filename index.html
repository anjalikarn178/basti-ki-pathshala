About Basti ki Pathshala

Our mission is to bridge the educational gap in urban slums and rural communities by providing accessible, quality education and skill development programs.

Community Impact

Reaching over 500+ children across 15 communities with quality education.

Qualified Teachers

Our dedicated team of 25+ volunteers and teachers ensure quality learning.

Success Stories

100+ students have successfully transitioned to formal education systems.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive educational programs tailored to community needs.

Basic Education

Fundamental literacy and numeracy programs for children and adults.

Digital Literacy

Computer skills and digital awareness programs for the modern world.

Skill Development

Vocational training programs to enhance employability and entrepreneurship.

Health Awareness

Health education and hygiene awareness programs for families.

Women Empowerment

Special programs focused on women's education and skill development.

Community Support

Holistic support including counseling and family guidance programs.
